LAKESITE Education School

The school was envisioned by the proprietor to be a model institution based on the foundation of academics, co-curricular activities, and community service. It is Rophine’s mission to provide every student with a challenging education that will develop critical thinking skills, strong character, and an appreciation for diversity. As an academy dedicated to academic merit and personal relationships, we value our parents and students. Our school is a remarkable institution. It represents a community of people coming together around a shared vision, connected by purpose and values.

We have consistently produced the best candidates with 400 marks who have joined the best national schools countrywide. Some of our students have joined prestigious universities and are professionals in various fields locally and abroad .

Motto

Achieving excellence

H

Achieving excellence means always striving to do your best in everything you do. It means setting goals, working hard, and never giving up, even when faced with challenges. It's about constantly learning and growing, and being open to new opportunities. Having a positive attitude and believing in yourself is key, as is managing your time wisely. Remember, achieving excellence is not about being perfect, but rather about giving your best effort and reaching your highest potential. we aim to create a brighter future for all students.

Vision

To provide high quality affordable education and to develop well rounded, responsible and confident learners.

V

The goal of providing high-quality affordable education is to ensure that all students have access to a great education without it being too expensive. It means offering excellent teaching and learning resources, experienced teachers, and a supportive learning environment, regardless of a student's financial background. The aim is to develop well-rounded learners who possess a wide range of knowledge, skills, and experiences.

Mission

To provide great opportunities for all learners to realize their full potential

M

To provide great opportunities for all learners to realize their full potential means that we want every student to have a chance to do their very best and become the best version of themselves. It means that we want to give you lots of different opportunities to learn and grow, whether it's in your classes, through sports, arts, or other activities. We believe that each one of you has unique talents and abilities. Our goal is to help you become confident, responsible individuals who can achieve amazing things
