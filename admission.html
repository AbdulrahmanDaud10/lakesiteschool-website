Our Philosphy

Lakesite values Respect, Learning and Working together. We foster an inclusiveness that celebrates diversity, honesty and equity. We hold high expectations of all staff and students and celebrate a strong commitment to hard work and personal achievement.

Academics Principle

The Lakesite school uses a comprehensive, intentional, and proactive approach to develop character.Schools committed to character development look at all they are doing through a character lens. They weave character into every aspect of the school culture.

Key of Success

To build greater trust between home and school, school leaders reach out and encourage parents and family members to be involved in the work of the school’s Character Committee.In addition, schools and families vigorously recruit stakeholders from the wider.

Lakesite Application Process

01.

Apply Online

Our admissions process begins when you complete an online application form. After you submit your application, you will be given an Applicant ID, which will serve as your reference number in future contact.

02.

Reception of Application

One of our Admissions Associates will acknowledge receipt of your application and confirm whether space is available in the school and/or grade level in which you have expressed interest.

03.

School Tour & Assessment

An interview and assessment will be scheduled for you and your child. The purpose of this evaluation is to gain insight into the student as a whole so that we can evaluate how best to nurture and grow your learner at Lakesite

04.

Confirmation

Following the assessment, one of our Admissions Officers will contact you to let you know if you've been admitted.

05.

Fee payment

Upon admission you will be required to make the prerequisite fee payment.
