Kindergaten SchoolThe kindergarten section of our school offers Baby Class (2-4 years), Pre-primary 1 (4-5 years) & Pre-primary 2 (5-6 years) at the end of which the pupils graduate to primary school. our way of teaching is child centered which encourages interactive classes that fosters creativity and child development.

Why Lakesite kindergarten?

Lakesite Kindergarten is an exceptional institution, We are a modern school with modern facilities including spacious classrooms, well-designed playgrounds, and adequate washrooms for boys and girls. Our teachers are highly qualified with great skills in teaching and learning.

Our curriculum is based on the framework provided by KNEC. We have embraced the Competency Based Curriculum from Pre-Primary One to Pre-Primary Two and onwards to our Primary School Grade 1- Grade 6.

Why Academics Works

At lakesite School Kindergarten we are proud of our UpperPriing inclusive ethos where we set high standards and expectations for our learners. From the moment a new St Bakhita learner joins our school community, we want them to feel a sense of belonging as this is essential to their future success.

We offer the Competency-Based Curriculum to our young learners as we nurture the values and the core competencies CBC emphasizes on.

Our Curriculum;

At lakesite School – Kindergarten your child will begin to learn basic skills like learning to do things for themselves, by exploring, investigating, watching and listening, talking, discussing, creating, communicating – in other words, playing. Play is children’s work.

As your child develops, so does their curiosity about the world around them. At Rophine we offer a safe and UpperPriing environment that allows them to explore, and learn in their own unique way. Our children learn and grow at their own pace.

Our Philosphy

Lakesite values Respect, Learning and Working together. We foster an inclusiveness that celebrates diversity, honesty and equity. We hold high expectations of all staff and students and celebrate a strong commitment to hard work and personal achievement.
